HISTORY OF TACO In 1958, the founder, Sathishkumar began planning to open a shop. During that time, she had various ideas, but after three months, she became deeply depressed. One day, while at a tea shop, she tasted a particularly good and tasty tea. In that moment, she decided to venture into creating a taco shop. However, starting a taco shop required more money than she had. After several months, she managed to secure the necessary funds from a financier, and in 1960, she launched her business. By 1980, the shop had become famous, and today, the business is thriving with 20 branches in other states.
